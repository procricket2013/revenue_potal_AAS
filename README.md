## Overview
The Revenue Portal AAS (Application as a Service) consists of two main modules: the Student Portal and the Admin Portal. This document outlines the key features, requirements, and goals of both portals.

## Portal 1: Student Portal
The Student Portal is designed to assist students in managing their academic and administrative tasks efficiently. It provides a user-friendly interface for students to interact with various services related to their education.

### Features
- **User Registration and Login**: Secure user registration and login functionality.
- **Profile Management**: Students can update their personal information and manage their profiles.
- **Course Management**: View and enroll in courses, view course materials, and track progress.
- **Examination Management**: Access examination schedules, results, and submit feedback.
- **Payment Processing**: Facilitate secure payments for tuition and fees.
- **Communication**: Receive notifications and communicate with faculty via an integrated messaging system.

### Requirements
- Users must have a valid email address for registration.
- The system must support various browsers and mobile devices.

### Goals
- Provide an intuitive and efficient platform for students.
- Enhance communication between students and faculty.
- Streamline administrative processes for students.

## Portal 2: Admin Portal
The Admin Portal is designed for administrative staff to manage the overall operations of the educational institution. It provides tools for monitoring and facilitating student activities.

### Features
- **User Management**: Add, edit, and remove user accounts (students and faculty).
- **Course Management**: Create, update, and manage courses and enrollments.
- **Examination Management**: Create and manage examination schedules and results.
- **Financial Management**: Monitor and manage payment transactions.
- **Reporting**: Generate reports on student performance, enrollment statistics, and financial data.

### Requirements
- Admin users must have appropriate permissions to access the portal.
- The system must provide role-based access control.

### Goals
- Improve operational efficiency of administrative tasks.
- Ensure data integrity and security within the system.
- Provide comprehensive reporting tools for better decision-making.
